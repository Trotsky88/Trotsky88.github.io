In my spare time I enjoy buying parts offline and putting together drones and computers. Ultimate Frisbee

For my first two years of college I played for the Montana State ultimate frisbee team. We traveled all over the mid west to play all sorts of college teams. Unfortunately I had to drop out of the team when I broke my arm my Sophmore year.

Greek Life

During my Sophmore year of college I was given the opportunity to start the Iota Epsilon chapter of Alpha Sigma Phi out at Montana State. This was a lot of fun and gave me the opportunity to become more involved with the school and Bozeman as a whole. During my three years in the fraternity I was given the roles of Director of Philanthropy and Director of Fundraising. These positions helped me learn how to raise and manage money as well as what the best forms of comunity service are for your local comunity.
